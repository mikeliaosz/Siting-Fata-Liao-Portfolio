About

Statement

Liao Fata is a fashion design student based between China and Chicago. Her work explores memory, distance, and emotional connection through garments and crafted surfaces.

Working across draping, structure development, embroidery, fabric manipulation, and illustration, she treats clothing as a vessel for storytelling—where personal cultural symbols meet contemporary silhouettes.

Her collection A Tranquil Night (静夜思) draws from lunar imagery and homesickness, translating the quiet intimacy of longing into tactile form.
